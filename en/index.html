■ Campaign Terms & Conditions

Because this campaign offers highly rare items at exceptional prices, participation is limited to customers who meet all of the following criteria.

  • Subscribers-Only Campaign
    This campaign is exclusively for Shopkloset subscription members. Please note that non-subscribers are not eligible to participate.
  • Mandatory "Unboxing" Post
    As a condition of winning, you are required to post an "unboxing" video or photo on your personal social media account (YouTube / Instagram / X / TikTok) upon receiving the item. Please be aware that if you do not post the unboxing content, or if we are unable to verify your post, you will be required to return the item in accordance with our policy.
  • Product Quality & Return Policy
    All items are guaranteed authentic and have been professionally verified. However, as these are special campaign items, we do not accept returns or claims of any kind regarding the product condition. Please acknowledge this before entering.
  • Verification Process
    Winners will receive a verification email from us. You must reply to this email with the social media account handle you intend to use. If you do not currently have a social media account, you will be required to create one to participate.
  • Content Usage Rights
    By participating, you grant Shopkloset permission to use your post (including screenshots and media assets) for our advertising, official website, and other promotional materials.
  • Winners are eligible to purchase the item for 1 JPY. However, please note that depending on your country of residence, customs duties and import taxes may apply. Any such charges are the sole responsibility of the purchaser.
